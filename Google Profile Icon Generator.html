How to Set Your Google Profile Icon

  1. Download the image by clicking the "Download Icon" button.
  2. Sign in to your Google Account.
  3. Open your Google Account settings by clicking your profile picture in the top-right corner and selecting "Manage your Google Account."
  4. Under the "Personal info" tab, click on the profile icon or the current profile picture.
  5. Click on "Set Profile Picture" or "Change" (if you already have a profile picture).
  6. Select the downloaded image from your device and click "Open."
  7. Adjust the cropping if necessary, then click "Done" to save your new profile icon.
